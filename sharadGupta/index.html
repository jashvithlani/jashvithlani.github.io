Saved Gravity Bath Collection: Expertise Rescues Building
We successfully completed a retrofitting project at Gravity Bath Collection in Sahibabad, rescuing the building from potential disaster. Despite challenges caused by an unskilled contractor, we were able to rectify the sagging slab due to improper concrete mixing. Our intervention not only saved the building but also saved our client nearly 1.5 Crore. This project stands as a testament to our commitment to quality and expertise in retrofitting solutions.

Strategic Collaboration: Avoiding Unnecessary Costs in New Friends Colony
We recently collaborated with a client in New Friends Colony, where the initial consultant recommended sheet piling along the entire foreside. Upon our involvement and coordination with the structure consultant, we were able to avoid unnecessary piling, resulting in a significant cost saving of 30%. This project highlights our commitment to cost-effective solutions and strategic collaboration to achieve optimal results for our clients.

Meet SHARAD gupta

   M.Tech Structure, Construction Experts & Creater of CETS Framwork   

Mr. Sharad Gupta has more than 25 years of expertise in Structural Design and Consultancy.

He is a highly experienced professional who has successfully completed 5000 projects across India and abroad as a designer, and has also undertaken nearly 50 projects as a contractor within India.

He began his career as a Structural Engineer and, driven by an unwavering commitment to excellence, founded Sanrachna Consultants in 1999.

Today, his visionary leadership plays a pivotal role in shaping the future of structural engineering, as Sanrachna Consultants merges with Sanrachna Solutions LLP to form Sanrachna Project India Pvt. Ltd. Mr. Sharad Gupta's journey is marked by dedication, innovation, and a profound commitment to safety and sustainability.
